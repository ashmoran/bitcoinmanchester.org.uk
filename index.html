Bitcoin Manchester

Website is work in progress! The mailing list link is at the bottom of the page.

What?

Bitcoin Manchester is a meetup for Bitcoin users, miners, buyers, sellers or anyone else otherwise interested in Bitcoin in the north-west.

Where?

Bitcoin Manchester's first meetup will be at the MadLab on Edge Street in the Northern Quarter map.

When?

The first meeting will be on Monday, June 24th 2013, 7-9pm (roughly). Come along!

More info
